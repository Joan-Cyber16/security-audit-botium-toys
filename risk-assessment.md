# Risk Assessment

## Risk Description
Botium Toys currently lacks proper asset management and control implementation, increasing exposure to cybersecurity threats. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Risk Score
8/10 (High Risk)

## Key Issues
- No asset inventory or classification
- No encryption for customer data
- No IDS system
- No backups or disaster recovery plan
- Weak password policies
- Excessive user access to sensitive data

## Impact
- Data breaches (PII/SPII exposure)
- Financial penalties (compliance violations)
- Operational disruption
